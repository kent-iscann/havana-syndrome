## [2026-04-13] ingest | NASEM Report, CDC Report, Canadian Diplomats Study

**Three major source documents ingested:**

### NASEM (Dec 2020) — Directed RF "most plausible"
- 19-member multidisciplinary committee, commissioned by State Department
- Acute symptoms: "unlike any disorder in neurological or general medical literature"
- Frey Effect confirmed as auditory mechanism for pulsed RF
- PPPD in ~25% of NIH patients (functional, not psychiatric)
- Key recommendations: real-time RF monitoring, baseline health data

### CDC Final Report (Dec 2019) — Completely inconclusive
- 95 cases reviewed (15 presumptive, 31 possible, 49 ruled out)
- 11-month delay before State Dept provided records
- No mechanism, cause, or treatment identified

### Friedman/Dalhousie (2019) — Organophosphate neurotoxicity
- 26 Canadian diplomats — BBB leakage, white matter degradation, OP detection
- Directly contradicts NASEM's chemical dismissal
- Canadian cohort lacked acute directional symptoms of U.S. cases
