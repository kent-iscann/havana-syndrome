## Overview

Researchers at the University of Miami (Hoffer et al., 2019) provided clinical data on U.S. Embassy personnel to the NASEM committee. Their data was one of the four primary sources used in the 2020 NASEM assessment.

## Clinical Data (Hoffer et al., 2019)

### Key Findings
- **25 of 25** patients in Miami dataset reported perceived loud sound
- **23 of 25** reported chronic dizziness
- Provided one of the largest datasets for the NASEM clinical summary

### NASEM Assessment
- Used as one of four primary clinical data sources
- Committee noted limitations: patients evaluated at vastly different time intervals post-onset
