## Overview

GRU Unit 29155 is a Russian military intelligence unit implicated as a prime suspect in directing or facilitating the anomalous health incidents affecting U.S. and allied personnel worldwide.

## Evidence and Connections

### 2024 Joint Investigation
A joint investigation by **60 Minutes, The Insider, and Der Spiegel** (March 2024) linked Unit 29155 to AHI incidents:
- Members identified at or near AHI incident locations
- Unit linked to **"non-lethal acoustic weapons"** development and awards
- Known for involvement in the **Salisbury poisoning** and other covert operations

### Travel Tracking
Mobile phone tracking data placed Unit 29155 members in close physical proximity to CIA targets in:
- **Poland**
- **Georgia** — **Albert Averyanov** (son of GRU deputy chief) identified by a victim outside her home during an attack
- **Australia**
- **Taiwan**

### Notable Individuals
- **Albert Averyanov** — Son of GRU deputy chief, identified by victim in Georgia
- Unit leadership reportedly linked to the Russian Defense Ministry's 2012 announcement of "psychotronic" weapon development plans
