## Overview

The NIH conducted clinical studies on AHI patients under its Clinical Center program, providing the most comprehensive biomedical examination of Havana Syndrome victims.

## Studies

### JAMA Publication (March 2024)
Five-year study of approximately 80 current and former U.S. officials:
- **No significant differences** in brain structure or function between AHI patients and control groups
- **No MRI-detectable brain injury** or biological abnormalities found
- Failed to replicate the 2018 University of Pennsylvania study
- Higher rates of **imbalance, fatigue, PTSD, and depression** among AHI reporters

### Study Shutdown
- Studies were **subsequently shut down** after an internal investigation revealed some participants had been **coerced to join**
- Critics cite this as validating victim skepticism toward NIH methodology
