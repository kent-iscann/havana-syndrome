## Overview

The ODNI leads U.S. intelligence community coordination and produces the Annual Threat Assessment. It has been the primary body issuing official assessments on Anomalous Health Incidents.

## Assessments

### 2023 Assessment
- **Seven agencies** concluded foreign adversary involvement is **"very unlikely"**
- **"No credible evidence"** found linking a foreign actor to any specific AHI
- **"Many unanswered questions"** due to research gaps
- ODNI focused on a **subset of priority cases**

### 2025 Update
- **Five agencies** maintained "very unlikely" stance
- **Two agencies** shifted to "roughly even chance" (low confidence):
  - Agency A (reportedly NSA): Assessed "roughly even chance" a foreign country used a novel weapon
  - Agency B: Assessed "roughly even chance" a foreign actor *possesses* such capability
- Intelligence shows foreign directed energy research **"making progress"**
- All IC components agreed no compelling intelligence tied a foreign actor to specific events
