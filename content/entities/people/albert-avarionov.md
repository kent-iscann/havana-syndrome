## Overview

Albert Avarionov is the son of the commander of **GRU Unit 29155** and was identified by a victim as present at the scene of an AHI incident in Tbilisi, Georgia, in 2021.

## The Tbilisi Incident (2021)

- A U.S. official's wife in Georgia (**Tbilisi**) was hit by a **"piercing sound"** outside her home
- Immediately after the incident, she saw a man outside her property
- She later identified this man from a photograph as **Albert Avarionov**
- Avarionov is the son of the commander of GRU Unit 29155

## Significance

This is one of the most direct piece of evidence linking Russian intelligence to a specific AHI incident:
- **Specific individual identification** by a victim at time of attack
- Connection to Unit 29155 commander (hereditary intelligence link)
- Occurred alongside other confirmed AHI incidents in Georgia
