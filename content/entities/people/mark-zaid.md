## Overview

Mark Zaid is an attorney who represents **over two dozen victims** of Anomalous Health Incidents. He has been a vocal advocate for victims and a critic of the U.S. government's handling of the crisis.

## Cover-Up Allegations

Zaid claims there is **"without a doubt, evidence of a cover-up"**:

> "What I've seen more so is, 'We see lines of inquiry that would take us potentially to answers we don't want to have to deal with, so we're not going to explore any of those avenues.'"

## Perspective

Zaid's position reflects frustration with the intelligence community's reluctance to pursue lines of investigation that could lead to uncomfortable conclusions about Russian involvement and whether AHIs constitute an act of war.
