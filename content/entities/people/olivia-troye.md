## Overview

Olivia Troye, former homeland security and counterterrorism advisor to **Vice President Mike Pence**, is one of the highest-profile victims of Anomalous Health Incidents, having reported symptoms after an incident **outside the White House**.

## Incident Details

- Affected near the **Eisenhower Executive Office Building** in the White House complex
- Symptoms: Vertigo, severe ear pain described as a **"sharp pencil" poking the eardrum**
- Public testimony included in 60 Minutes report

## Significance

Troye's case is notable because:
- She is a **high-ranking official** in the White House complex
- She has been **public** about her experience
- Her incident occurred on **U.S. soil** near the center of government

## Quote

> "It was like this piercing feeling on the side of my head... I got like, vertigo. I was unsteady, I was, I felt nauseated, I was somewhat disoriented."
