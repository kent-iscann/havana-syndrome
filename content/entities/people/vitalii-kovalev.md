## Overview

Vitalii Kovalev is a Russian military electrical engineer with top-secret clearance who immigrated to the United States and worked as a chef. He is a key person of interest in the Havana Syndrome investigation.

## Background

- Russian military electrical engineer with **top-secret clearance**
- Immigrated to the U.S. to work as a chef
- **2020:** Arrested after a high-speed chase in Florida

### Evidence Found
- Device in his car to **erase GPS data**
- Notes on bank accounts
- These items were inconsistent with a simple electrical engineer/chef profile

### Interrogation
- Interviewed for **80 hours** by FBI Agent "Carrie"
- Returned to Russia
- Reportedly **killed in Ukraine in 2023**

## Significance

Kovalev represents a **direct link** between Russian military intelligence and individuals present on U.S. soil with capabilities inconsistent with their stated profession. His death in Ukraine (2023) eliminated a potential source of intelligence.
