## Overview

Greg Edgreen, a retired Army Lieutenant Colonel, ran the **Defense Intelligence Agency's investigation** into Anomalous Health Incidents from 2021–2023.

## Role in AHI Investigation

Edgreen stated publicly for the first time that he believes **Russia is responsible** for AHIs.

### Key Finding — Victim Selection Pattern
Edgreen revealed that victims were consistently the **"top 5-10% performing officers"** who specifically worked **against Russian interests**. This suggests:
- **Targeted neutralization** rather than random harassment
- Strategic goal of removing the most effective U.S. officers
- Covert incapacitation through traumatic brain injury

### Criticism of U.S. Government
Edgreen suggests the U.S. government set an **"impossibly high evidentiary bar"** to avoid facing "hard truths," such as whether these attacks constitute an act of war.
