## Overview

The Hotel Nacional de Cuba, a historic luxury hotel in Havana. On August 22, 2017, two CIA "temporary duty" (TDY) intelligence officers fell ill at this hotel with symptoms matching earlier AHI victims.

## Significance

- The **last straw** that led the CIA to close its Havana station
- Occurred months after the initial embassy cluster, suggesting an **ongoing threat**
- The ARB confirmed these as injuries matching the pattern
- The incident was kept secret until revealed in the declassified ARB report
