## Overview

U.S. Consulate in Guangzhou, China. In May 2018, an employee reported AHI symptoms matching the pattern of earlier Havana victims — the first confirmed case outside Cuba.

## Significance

- **First confirmed offshore-Cuba case** — proved the phenomenon was not Cuba-specific
- Symptoms were described as **"entirely consistent"** with Havana victims
- Marked a shift in understanding AHIs from a regional Cuba issue to a **global threat**
- The ARB confirmed this as a verified case of the same phenomenon
