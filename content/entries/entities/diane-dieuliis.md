# Diane DiEuliis — Center for the Study of Weapons of Mass Destruction

**Type:** Person — NDU/Centers and Institutes Researcher
**Updated:** 2026-04-13

## Overview

Diane DiEuliis is a researcher at the Center for the Study of Weapons of Mass Destruction, Institute of National Strategic Studies, National Defense University.

## Publications

### Giordano & DiEuliis (2022)
"Anomalous Health Incidents of the Havana Syndrome: Implications and Lessons for Global Biosecurity and Defense" — published at NDU Digital Commons.

Key thesis: AHIs connect to broader national security concerns about **novel biological weapons** and **dual-use technologies** that "complicate detection, attribution, and treatment."

Established that:
- ~2 dozen original Havana 2016 cases are verified physical injury
- Directed energy is a valid possibility
- Technologies are sufficiently advanced to produce and operationally employ such devices
