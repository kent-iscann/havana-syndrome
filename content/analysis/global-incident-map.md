## Overview

This page provides a systematic geographic and temporal mapping of all documented Anomalous Health Incidents worldwide, from 2014 to 2026. Understanding the global pattern is critical to evaluating hypotheses: **if incidents cluster at intelligence hubs and target specific personnel, it supports directed weapons; if they correlate with environmental factors (Zika fumigation campaigns), it supports chemical exposure.**

## Hypothesis Evaluation

### Does the pattern support directed weapons?
- **YES:** Clusters at intelligence hubs, specific targeting of U.S./Canadian personnel, temporal correlation with GRU travel, no local civilian cases
- **YES:** Domestic cases target specific high-value individuals (Polymeropoulos in Russia, "Chris" in Virginia, FBI Agent "Carrie")

### Does the pattern support chemical exposure?
- **NO:** Only Cuba had aggressive Zika fumigation
- **NO:** Incidents in Vienna, D.C., Georgia, Australia, Taiwan lack environmental chemical explanation
- **PARTIAL:** Cuba cluster could be explained by chemicals, but global spread cannot

### Does the pattern support mass psychogenic illness?
- **PARTIAL:** Cluster pattern in Cuba could be MPI
- **NO:** Global spread across isolated embassies with no inter-personnel contact doesn't support social contagion
- **NO:** Directional, room-specific features difficult to explain psychogenically

## Key Insight

The **single most important geographic pattern** is that incidents cluster in locations where:
1. **U.S./Canadian intelligence personnel** are posted
2. **Russian intelligence services** have significant operational capability
3. The target's **work specifically opposes Russian interests**

This pattern is consistent across Cuba, Vienna, Moscow, Georgia, and potentially domestic U.S. cases. It is **inconsistent with random environmental factors, local disease, or mass psychogenic illness**.
