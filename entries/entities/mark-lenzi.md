# Mark Lenzi — State Department Officer, AHI Victim, Whistleblower

**Type:** Person — Former State Department Officer
**Updated:** 2026-04-06

## Overview

Mark Lenzi is a former U.S. State Department officer who reportedly suffered Anomalous Health Incidents and has been a vocal critic of the U.S. government's handling of the crisis and intelligence assessments.

## Statements

Regarding the ODNI's 2025 assessment split:
> "This see-no-evil ridiculous assessment not only covers for Russia but proves the point of the recent HPSCI report."

Lenzi believes the intelligence community is suppressing evidence implicating Russia in AHI attacks.

## Role

Lenzi serves as both a victim and an unofficial spokesperson for the community of AHIs who feel the government has not adequately investigated or acknowledged foreign adversary responsibility.
